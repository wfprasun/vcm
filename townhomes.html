Project Details:

The client simply attached a plan (1800sf Townhome) and a prefered units of measure sheet in an email and asked for us to present an approximate timeframe to estimate the following categories:

  • Foundation
  • Shaft wall
  • Lumber
  • Roofing
  • Windows
  • Exterior and interior doors
  • Trim – baseboard, window casing
  • Sheetrock boards
  • Flooring
  • Exterior cladding material
Pricing:

As per the requirements to complete the take-offs:

  • 6 Hours for Review of the drawings
  • 40 Hours for Take-offs of all listed items
  • 4 Hours for Compilation and preparation of summary spreadsheet
  • 50 Hrs Total

50 Hours (estimate) x $35/hour = $1,750

Results:

The client assigned us to take-off 3 different model townhomes. We were able to complete the take-offs for one townhome every week for 3 weeks straight. It took us 40 hours for each townhome take-off. A total of 120 hours over 3 weeks.
